Call Me Taz

Hi, I'm Taz, a budding developer with a passion for technology. For the past 9 years I have been working in the Hospitality Industry, however I am undertaking the Coder Academy Fast Track Bootcamp course in order to upskill and change careers to become a Software Developer. I have previously undertaken a Cert 3 in Media, which I feel gives me a solid foundation for studing code. I am also very much a 'girly-girl' by stereotypical standards, and have also studied Hairdressing and Make-up, gaining a Diploma of Specialist Make-up Services. I want to showcase that being a Woman in Tech can have many different appearances, and that just because someone is girly and may look like a 'bimbo', that doesn't mean they don't have the capacity to learn how to code. I am also incredibly passionate about animals, having rescued a fair few over the years. A particular passion of mine is reptiles, which is largely due to how misunderstood they are. I would love to use tech to educate people on how we can help facilitate healthy animal keeping and breeding in Australia, as well as globally.
